Questions?

Frequently Asked

Is there a registration fee?
Yes, a one-time registration fee is required to prevent spam and ensure serious workers. The fee amount is displayed on the registration page. Your fee is reviewed along with your account.
When can I withdraw my earnings?
Withdrawal is unlocked after you complete and get approved for all 10 tasks in your current task cycle. Once approved, you can request a withdrawal for your available balance.
How do I get more tasks after completing 10?
After completing your 10 tasks, you need to contact admin to reset your tasks. Task resets are only available after 24 hours from your last completed task, and require manual admin approval.
What are Special Events?
Special Events are exclusive tasks assigned to specific users. If you have been assigned a Special Event, you will see it in your dashboard and receive a notification. These carry higher reward amounts.
