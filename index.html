Anti-Abuse & Verification

Protected by multi-layer verification

FreeWork enforces strict anti-spam rules to protect campaign integrity and prevent reward abuse.

🔒
1 Account Per Identity
1 email = 1 account. 1 phone = 1 account. 1 payout wallet = 1 account. Duplicates are flagged and banned automatically.
🌐
IP & Device Monitoring
Repeat IPs trigger review (+20). Same IP across 3+ accounts = Spam Risk (+50). Same device fingerprint = flagged (+60).
✅
Admin Approval Required
All users must be manually approved by admin before accessing any tasks. No auto-approvals in default configuration.
⏱️
Daily Task Limit
Users cannot submit more than 10 tasks in a rolling 24-hour window. Trusted users may receive admin-granted custom limits.
📸
Proof Verification
Every submission requires screenshot proof reviewed by admin. Duplicate or fake screenshots are flagged (+70 risk score).
📊
Risk Score System
Each user carries a live risk score. 0–19: Low. 20–49: Review. 50–79: High Risk. 80+: Spam. Alerts notify admin instantly.
⚠️ FreeWork rewards are paid only for approved task submissions. Fake accounts, duplicate accounts, fake screenshots, misleading claims, or abuse may lead to permanent suspension. Crypto payments and payouts must use the correct coin, network, and wallet address.
🚨 Crypto payments are final. If funds are sent to the wrong wallet or wrong network, they may be lost permanently.
